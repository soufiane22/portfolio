Production Management Mobile Application

Description

Android application installed on tablets used by production team leads for the declaration of production parameter like working hours, Output,Scrap for each fabrication order.

Key Features :
  • Real-Time Visibility: Gain instant insights into team performance and production progress, enabling swift adjustments and proactive problem-solving.
  • Simplified Data Management: Eliminate manual paperwork and ensure accurate, easily accessible records for informed decision-making.
  • Enhanced Communication: Facilitate streamlined communication between team leads and managers, fostering collaboration and optimizing workflow.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Benefit from an intuitive and mobile-optimized design, empowering efficient data entry and analysis
Benefits:
  • Reduced Costs: Minimize errors and streamline data management for significant cost savings.
  • Improved Quality: Ensure consistent production quality with accurate parameter tracking and timely adjustments.
  • Empowered Team Leads: Equip team leads with the tools to effectively manage their teams and optimize output.
