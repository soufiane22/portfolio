Production Management Web Application

Description

The purpose of this software is to manage the production process and track the productivity of manufacturing lines the evolution of Orders of Fabrication (OFs) and the presence of employees, It is primarily used by production managers and supervisors, and the data is shared with an Android application installed on a tablet used by production team leads. The platform enables swift decision-making and proactive adjustments to optimize workflow efficiency.

Key Features :
  • Manage Production Process : Effortlessly track the progress of Orders of Fabrication (OFs),and ensure smooth production flow.
  • Tracking the productivity of manufacturing lines.
  • Check the presence and working hours of employees.
  • Managing the groups of employees.
Benefits :
  • Facilitate monitoring of production and SCRAP levels in the manufacturing lines.
  • Facilitate access to the archived data.
  • Reduce the budget for purchasing materials (paper, printers, and ink)
